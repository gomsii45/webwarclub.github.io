About The Event

ABOUT THE EVENT

“Technocrats” the computer club of SRDAV Dayanand Vihar, annually organizes INTER WARTEX along with the teachers of the IT department. Every year the event witnesses a mass participation of over 600 students from classes IIIrd to XIIth from Delhi NCR schools.
The list of competitions includes- Web Designing, aptitude test, photoshop, Game designing, Brain Wars, programming and so much more.
The highlight of the event is Up-Vento, a competition to showcase the STEM skills of students wherein the students are required to design a working model of any innovation from waste material.
Our goal is to bring out hidden talent among students, a path that help them to take a leap from users of technology to creators and innovators.
