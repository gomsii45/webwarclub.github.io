About The Club

ABOUT THE CLUB

“Technocrats” the computer club of Dronachaya Group Of Institution, annually organizes WEB WAR CLUB along with our mentor Simranpreet Kaur (Assistant professor of CSE department). Every year the event witnesses a mass participation of over 300 students from Ist , IInd and IIIrd year from Dronacharya.
The list of competitions includes- Web Designing, aptitude test, photoshop, Game designing, Brain Wars, programming and so much more.
The highlight of the club is Up-Vento, a club to showcase the programming skills of students wherein the students can gain the knowledge ans share the thoghts.
Our goal is to bring out hidden talent among students, a path that help them to take a leap from users of technology to creators and innovators.
